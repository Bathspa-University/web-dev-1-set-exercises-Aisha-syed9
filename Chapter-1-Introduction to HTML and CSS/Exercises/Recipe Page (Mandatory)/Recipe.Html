How to make Creamy Garlic Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti
  • minced, 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped, fresh parsley (Can also add garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Stir in heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Reduce heat to low and stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Add cooked spaghetti to the pot and toss until well coated with the creamy garlic sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. If desired, garnish with finely chopped fresh parsley.
