Plot

The investigation into the disappearance of Will Byers, a young child, is at the heart of Stranger Things' central mystery. Unbelievably amazing events are uncovered while his friends, family, and the local police carry out search efforts. They consist of secret government experiments, encounters with an enigmatic girl with psychokinetic skills, and the finding of the Upside Down, a different dimension.
