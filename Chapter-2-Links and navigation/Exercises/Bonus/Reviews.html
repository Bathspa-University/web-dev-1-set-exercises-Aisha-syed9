Reviews

Stranger Things has won numerous accolades from critics because of its heartfelt allusions, excellent acting, and compelling narrative.

A tribute to 1980s pop culture, a captivating setting, and skillful character development have all won the show accolades.

Several Primetime Emmy Awards are among the many accolades the show has won.

Both reviewers and viewers have commended its distinctive fusion of science fiction, mystery, and horror, indicating its enduring appeal.
