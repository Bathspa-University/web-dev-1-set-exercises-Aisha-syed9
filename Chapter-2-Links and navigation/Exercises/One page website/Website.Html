Overview

Science fiction and horror elements coexist in the highly regarded television series Stranger Things by The Duffer Brothers. When it debuted on Netflix in July 2016 it garnered a global following.

A group of young people are engaged in a search for their missing friend at the center of the story which takes place in the 1980s in the fictitious Indiana town of Hawkins. They investigate enigmatic government plans and come across unearthly occurrences while pursuing them.

Plot

The investigation into the disappearance of Will Byers a young child is at the heart of Stranger Things central mystery. Unbelievably amazing events are uncovered while his friends family and the local police carry out search efforts. They consist of secret government experiments encounters with an enigmatic girl with psychokinetic skills and the finding of the Upside Down a different dimension.

Reviews

Stranger Things has won numerous accolades from critics because of its heartfelt allusions excellent acting and compelling narrative.

A tribute to 1980s pop culture a captivating setting and skillful character development have all won the show accolades.

Several Primetime Emmy Awards are among the many accolades the show has won.

Both reviewers and viewers have commended its distinctive fusion of science fiction mystery and horror indicating its enduring appeal.
